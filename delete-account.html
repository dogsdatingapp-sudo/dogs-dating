Delete Your Dogs Dating Account

If you would like to delete your Dogs Dating account and associated data, you can do so directly from the mobile application.

Delete from the app

Open the Dogs Dating app → Profile → Delete Account

This will permanently remove your account, profile information, dogs, images and chats from our systems.

Unable to access the app?

If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by contacting us from your registered email address.

Email: dogs.dating.app@gmail.com

We will process the deletion request and remove associated data.
